Below is an example of my favorite exercise, the dumbbell press. This shows the muscle groups it is targeting, along with the motion involved.

Three things to focus on when doing dumbbell press:

  1. Lay flat on bench, use feet as base.
  2. Retract shoulders, make sure lower back is not on bench.
  3. Contract and stretch chest when lowering and raising weight.
